🌍 Fun Facts About Papua New Guinea

🏞 Destinations

  • PNG is home to over 600 islands, each with its own charm—from volcanic landscapes to pristine coral reefs.
  • The Kokoda Track is a world-famous trekking route, rich in WWII history and jungle adventure.
  • The Sepik River is one of the longest rivers in the South Pacific, lined with villages known for their intricate wood carvings.

🎭 Culture

  • PNG has over 850 languages, making it the most linguistically diverse country in the world.
  • The Mount Hagen Cultural Show brings together tribes from across the highlands, showcasing traditional dances, costumes, and rituals.
  • Shells were once used as currency in some regions, highlighting the deep connection between culture and nature.

🦜 Wildlife

  • PNG is the kingdom of the birds-of-paradise, with dazzling plumage and courtship dances found nowhere else.
  • The tree kangaroo is a rare marsupial that lives in the cloud forests of the highlands.
  • Its waters host some of the world’s best diving spots, with vibrant coral reefs, WWII wrecks, and marine life like manta rays and turtles.
